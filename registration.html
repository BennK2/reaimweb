Registration & Delegate Information

Accreditation and attendance requirements

The Registration Journey

A simple step-by-step process for official delegations to secure their attendance.

Step 1: DAO Nomination

Each invited country must designate an Accreditation Officer (DAO). The DAO acts as the sole focal point for registering the entire national delegation.

Step 2: Delegate Registration

The DAO completes the official registration forms for all delegation members before the deadline. Late submissions will not be processed due to security clearance requirements.

Deadline:TBA (11:59 PM GMT+3)

Step 3: Travel & Visas

Review Kenya's entry requirements. Depending on nationality, an ETA or Visa may be required. Book accommodation early using recommended official hotels.

Step 4: Badge Collection

Upon arrival, DAOs collect the RFID-embedded badges for their delegation. Badges must be worn visibly at all times within the venue.

Restricted Access (Floaters)

Certain high-level events require special 'floaters' in addition to the standard badge.
