Quick Facts

Practical information to ensure a comfortable stay in Nairobi.

Weather

Average 20.5°C (68.9°F). Mild weather expected during April.

Standard Time

GMT+03:00 (East Africa Time).

Currency

Kenyan Shilling (KES). VISA, MasterCard, AMEX widely accepted.

Connectivity

220-240 Volts (Type G plug). High-speed Wi-Fi at all venues.

Emergencies

Ambulance & Fire: 999. Police: 999 or 112.

Medical

World-class private clinics and ambulance services available on call.
