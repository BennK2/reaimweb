Building on Global Norms

Following the foundational summits in The Hague (2023), Seoul (2024), and A Coruña (2026), the Nairobi Summit represents a critical juncture in global military AI governance.

We bring together states, international institutions, academia, and civil society to establish non-binding political declarations and actionable blueprints for the responsible development and deployment of artificial intelligence.
